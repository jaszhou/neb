What is Lost & Found?
You can register your precious belongings here and the information will be saved in Nebulas blockchain permanently. Once you have register the item, you will receive a unique hashcode. Mark the hashcode on your item such as a waterbottle. In case you lose the item and lucky someone find it, the finder may leave you a message on the blockchain. You may say a big THANK-YOU to the finder or even send the finder some NAS token as reward.
